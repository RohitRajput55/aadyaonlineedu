Programme Overview

The M.DU B.Ed. (Bachelor of Education) is a 2-year NCTE-approved programme affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University. Designed in compliance with NEP 2020, this programme combines theoretical knowledge with practical teaching experience to develop competent education professionals for diverse learning environments.

Key Features:

  • Outcome-Based Education (OBE) framework
  • Triple specialization system
  • 20-week intensive school internship
  • Artificial Intelligence in Education module
  • Yoga and Value Education integration

Programme Structure

Total Duration 4 Semesters (2 Years)
Total Credits 104 Credits
Teaching Hours 1200+ Contact Hours
Internship 20 Weeks Minimum

Detailed Curriculum

First Year Curriculum

Foundation Courses (Semester I)

  • • Learner and Learning Process (EDU-110, 5 Credits)
  • • Indian Education System (EDU-120, 5 Credits)
  • • Language Across Curriculum (EDU-130, 4 Credits)

Pedagogical Studies (Semester II)

  • • Pedagogy of Core Subject I (5 Credits)
  • • Pedagogy of Core Subject II (5 Credits)
  • • Educational Evaluation (EDU-240, 4 Credits)

Second Year Curriculum

Advanced Studies (Semester III)

  • • Inclusive Education (EDU-310, 5 Credits)
  • • Educational Research (EDU-320, 5 Credits)
  • • Specialization Elective (5 Credits)

Practical Engagement (Semester IV)

  • • School Internship (20 Weeks, 10 Credits)
  • • Educational Research Project (5 Credits)
  • • Rural Immersion Programme (3 Credits)
