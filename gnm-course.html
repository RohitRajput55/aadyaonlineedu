GNM Programme Overview

The 3.5-Year GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery) programme is approved by the Indian Nursing Council (INC) and State Nursing Registration Council. This comprehensive course equips students with clinical expertise and compassionate care skills to excel in diverse healthcare settings.

Key Features:

  • Integrated theory-practice curriculum
  • 1400+ hours of clinical experience
  • Advanced simulation lab training
  • Community health immersion programme
  • NCLEX-oriented preparation modules
