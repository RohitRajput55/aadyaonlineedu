Programme Overview

The CRSU B.Ed. (Bachelor of Education) is a 2-year professional programme approved by NCTE (National Council for Teacher Education) and affiliated with Haryana State Council of Higher Education. Designed as per NEP 2020 guidelines, this programme equips students with pedagogical skills and subject expertise to excel in modern educational environments.

Key Features:

  • Choice-Based Credit System (CBCS) pattern
  • Dual specialization options
  • Mandatory school internship programme
  • ICT-integrated teaching methodologies
  • Focus on inclusive education practices

Programme Structure

Total Duration 4 Semesters (2 Years)
Total Credits 96 Credits
Teaching Hours 1000+ Contact Hours
Internship 16 Weeks Minimum

Detailed Curriculum

First Year Curriculum

Core Courses (Semester I)

  • • Childhood and Growing Up (EDU-101, 4 Credits)
  • • Contemporary India and Education (EDU-102, 4 Credits)
  • • Learning and Teaching (EDU-103, 4 Credits)

Pedagogical Courses (Semester II)

  • • Pedagogy of School Subject I (4 Credits)
  • • Pedagogy of School Subject II (4 Credits)
  • • Assessment for Learning (EDU-204, 4 Credits)

Second Year Curriculum

Specialization Courses (Semester III)

  • • Gender, School and Society (EDU-301, 4 Credits)
  • • Knowledge and Curriculum (EDU-302, 4 Credits)
  • • Optional Specialization Elective (4 Credits)

Practical Components (Semester IV)

  • • School Internship (16 Weeks, 8 Credits)
  • • Action Research Project (4 Credits)
  • • Community Outreach Programme (2 Credits)
